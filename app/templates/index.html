About MITRE ATT&CK

The MITRE ATT&CK framework is a globally accessible knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques based on real-world observations.

  • Tactics: The "why" - adversary's tactical goal
  • Techniques: The "how" - methods to achieve tactical goals
  • Sub-techniques: More specific descriptions of techniques
